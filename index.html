About Me

I wasn't always the AI enthusiast I am today. My journey began far from the complex world of machine learning and neural networks—rooted in commerce, where my focus was on numbers, economics, and business. But deep down, there was always a spark, a curiosity about technology, a desire to push the boundaries of what’s possible. It wasn’t an easy transition. With no formal background in mathematics or programming, I decided to dive headfirst into AI, knowing I had to learn everything from scratch. What began as fascination quickly turned into obsession. I struggled, I failed, but I never stopped. Now, I’m building my own models, from creating custom AI solutions to experimenting with AI’s potential for space exploration. I’m driven by the desire to not only succeed but to create something bigger than myself—something that could revolutionize the way we understand the universe. My story is still being written, and I’m just getting started..

Recent AI Projects

Building LLaMA 2 7B from Scratch September 10, 2024

Learn how I built the LLaMA 2 7B model from scratch, focusing on custom architecture and checkpoints loading.

Building LLaMA 2 7B was a challenging yet rewarding journey. Starting with a deep understanding of the Transformer architecture, I focused on replicating a large language model from the ground up. The hardest part was designing a custom architecture that could handle massive datasets and efficiently manage checkpoints. I also experimented with various optimizers and learned how to fine-tune models for specific tasks, which helped me grow significantly as a developer. Through this project, I not only deepened my knowledge of AI but also learned the importance of patience and persistence when working with large models.

Creating Nano GPT: A Minimal Transformer August 23, 2024

This project showcases how I implemented a Nano GPT model from scratch and trained it on custom data.

The Nano GPT project was a fascinating exploration into how minimalistic models can be powerful when optimized correctly. Implementing a GPT model from scratch allowed me to understand tokenization, embedding layers, and multi-head attention at a granular level. One of the major challenges I faced was in managing memory efficiency, especially with large datasets. I also explored different training methods, which helped me tailor the model for custom tasks. This project really helped me hone my skills in model optimization and better understand how even smaller models can perform efficiently.

Custom Transformer Model Development July 15, 2024

A deep dive into how I built and fine-tuned a custom Transformer model tailored for specific AI tasks.

Building a custom Transformer was one of the most complex and insightful experiences I’ve had. From designing multi-head attention mechanisms to implementing positional encoding, this project pushed me to think deeply about how to adjust and fine-tune models for very specific AI tasks. I gained hands-on experience in applying activation functions, loss functions, and experimenting with different optimizers to achieve better performance. Additionally, working with datasets for training the model enhanced my understanding of data preparation and tokenization, which has been invaluable for future projects.
